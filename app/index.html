— About me —

While looking for an idea for my career, I've tested many options. I spent several months working for a large corporation. This taught me how to organize my work-time and how to work in a team. Then, I was working for almost two years as an E-Commerce Specialist for two online stores. I've gained experience in SEO, advertising campaigns and social media services. While I was preparing banners, graphics and other advertising materials, I found that creating new things from scratch gives me the greatest satisfaction.

Finally, I came across the front-end and the opportunities this path has opened for me. They made me realise that front-end suits me. Everyday I learn something new and everyday I discover how much is still ahead of me ... But isn’t programming just about continuous learning?

My name’s Weronika and I’d like to 
become a front-end developer.
